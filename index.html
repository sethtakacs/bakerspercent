What Are Baker’s Percentages?

Baker’s percentages express ingredients as a percent of the flour weight. This allows easy scaling and consistency, whether baking one loaf or one hundred.

To use these calculators, you’ll need a kitchen scale. Choose a recipe, input your desired flour weight, and the calculator will do the rest. Each recipe includes a recommended weight for one unit of the finished product.
