Instructions

  1. Grate zucchini and squeeze out excess moisture.
  2. Mix with sugar, eggs, and oil.
  3. Add dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Pour into greased loaf pan.
  5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 55–60 minutes.
  6. Cool completely before slicing.
