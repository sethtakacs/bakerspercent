Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients and mix until shaggy.
  2. Autolyse 30 minutes, then knead briefly.
  3. Bulk ferment 2 hours, folding every 30 minutes.
  4. Divide into 3 pieces, pre-shape, and rest 20 min.
  5. Shape into baguettes, proof on couche 45–60 min.
  6. Score and bake at 475°F (245°C) with steam for 20–25 minutes.
