Baguette

Recommended flour: 500g

Calculated Ingredient Weights:

Water: — g

Salt: — g

Yeast: — g

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and water, rest for 30 min (autolyse).
  2. Add salt and yeast, knead lightly.
  3. Ferment 2–3 hours with 3 stretch and folds every 30 min.
  4. Divide dough into 3, pre-shape into logs.
  5. Rest 20 minutes, then shape into baguettes.
  6. Proof 45–60 minutes seam-side up on floured couche.
  7. Score and bake at 475°F (245°C) with steam for 22–26 minutes.
