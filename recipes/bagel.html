Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients and knead until stiff and smooth.
  2. Ferment 1 hour, then divide and shape into 12 bagels.
  3. Refrigerate overnight or proof 30–45 minutes.
  4. Boil 30–60 seconds per side in water with malt syrup.
  5. Bake at 450°F (230°C) for 15–20 minutes until golden.
