Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, honey, and yeast until combined.
  2. Rest dough 20 minutes.
  3. Add salt and olive oil, knead 8–10 minutes.
  4. Cover and ferment until doubled (1.5–2 hours).
  5. Shape and place in loaf pan.
  6. Let rise 30–45 minutes.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35–40 minutes.
  8. Let cool before slicing.
