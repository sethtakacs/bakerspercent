Recommended flour: 500g

Calculated Ingredient Weights:

Water: — g

Salt: — g

Yeast: — g

Honey: — g

Olive Oil: — g

Instructions

  1. Mix whole wheat flour, water, honey, and yeast.
  2. Let rest 30 minutes. Add salt and olive oil.
  3. Knead for 10 minutes.
  4. Let rise 1.5 hours, then shape into a loaf.
  5. Proof 45 minutes and bake at 375°F for 40 minutes.
