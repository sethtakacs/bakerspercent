Instructions

  1. Combine flour, water, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl.
  2. Let sit for 15 minutes (autolyse).
  3. Add salt and oil, then knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  4. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 1.5 hours.
  5. Punch down, shape into a loaf, and place in a greased pan.
  6. Let rise again for 30–45 minutes.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35–40 minutes.
  8. Cool before slicing.
